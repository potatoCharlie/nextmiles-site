Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, display name, and optional profile photo. During gameplay, we collect your approximate location (city-level) when you use the teleport feature, and your in-game position on the map. We also store chat messages you send to other players and to NPC characters.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the game experience, including:

  • Displaying your profile to nearby players
  • Enabling chat between players
  • Tracking quest progress and achievements
  • Showing relevant points of interest near your in-game location
  • Sending push notifications about game events (with your permission)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your username, display name, avatar, and in-game position are visible to other players near you in the game. Chat messages are only visible to participants of the conversation. We use Supabase for data storage and authentication, and OpenAI/Anthropic APIs for NPC conversations (messages are sent to AI providers for response generation).

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS). We use row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data. All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through your profile screen
  • Update or correct your information at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Opt out of push notifications through device settings

To delete your account, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.

6. Children's Privacy

Next Miles is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, for significant changes, through an in-app notification.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at contact@nextmiles.ai.
